Our Story

We are a start up company that is relatively new to the field. But what we bring to the travel industry is more than cheap prices and awesome bundles. Excursive was born out of the needs of travellers. We strive to re-set the one size fits all industry stard in terms of offers, pricing, bundling and especially support to a new approach tailored to individual needs. Because we believe that travelling is every human's right irrespective of their location, income, race, gender and choices in a one home we call Earth.

What Do We Do Different

Thanks to our home-brewed sophisticated AI software, we target every single customer with a fresh approach tailored to their needs. Our AI customer service representatives takes into consideration not only the personal details but also the expectations of our guests to offer them the best options available in their preferred times for travel. We do this in order to eliminate any and all small detail going unnoticed so as to maximize the guest satisfaction.
